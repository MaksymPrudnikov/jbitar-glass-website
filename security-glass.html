How it works

Two panes of glass bonded under heat and pressure with a tough PVB or SGP interlayer. Standard glass shatters and falls away in one hit. Laminated glass takes the hit, cracks, and keeps standing — breaking through means cutting and tearing a plastic membrane while making noise the whole time. Most attempts stop before that.
