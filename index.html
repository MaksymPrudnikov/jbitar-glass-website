Frameless vs. semi-frameless — what's the real difference?

Frameless has no metal border around the glass at all. Semi-frameless keeps a slim frame around the door only, which is usually the lower-cost option and still looks clean.

How long does a shower enclosure install take?

Usually a single visit — three to five hours depending on size, layout and hardware.

Do you do corner and neo-angle showers?

Yes — corner units with a return panel, and neo-angle layouts for tighter bathroom corners.

Do glass railings need a permit in Ontario?

Most guard installs above a set height do. We handle the supporting engineering documentation as part of the quote, so you're not chasing it separately.

Tempered or laminated for a balcony railing?

Laminated is generally required at height for guard glass, since it stays in place even if it cracks. We confirm exactly what your railing height requires before quoting.

Can railing glass match the rest of the house?

Yes — ordering it alongside your other glass keeps tint and thickness consistent throughout.

Every window Foggy window / IGU fix We replace the sealed unit — not the whole window

A foggy window almost never means a new window. The seal on the insulated glass unit (IGU) has failed and let moisture in — we replace just the sealed glass unit, matching the Low-E coating and gas fill, without touching the frame.

  • On-site measure of the existing sash and glazing pocket
  • Low-E coating and argon fill matched to the original spec
  • Energy Star–eligible replacement units where applicable
  • One unit or a whole house, done room by room
LOW-E COATED · ARGON FILLED · ENERGY STAR ELIGIBLE
Why does a window go foggy?

The seal between the two panes fails and lets moisture in — the glass itself is usually fine, only the seal has given out.

Do I need a whole new window if it's foggy?

Almost never. Replacing just the sealed unit is faster and far cheaper than a full window and frame replacement.

How long does IGU replacement take?

Most homes are done in a single day, one unit at a time, with no mess left inside.

Entry · Patio · Ground floor Security glass Laminated glass that stays in the frame when struck

Laminated security glass for entry doors, patio sliders and ground-floor windows. It looks like ordinary glass — the difference is entirely in what happens when someone tries to get through it: it cracks, but the interlayer holds it in the frame.

  • PVB or SGP laminated glass, sourced to spec from certified suppliers
  • Sized to fit existing frames — no structural changes needed
  • Optional acoustic upgrade — the same laminate cuts outside noise
  • UV-filtering interlayer slows fading of floors and furniture
LAMINATED PVB/SGP · UV FILTERING · SOUND DAMPENING
Does security glass look different from regular glass?

No — same clarity, same appearance. The difference is entirely in what happens when it's struck.

Which windows should I prioritize first?

Entry-door glazing and ground-floor windows — the most common points of entry, and the highest-impact upgrade.
